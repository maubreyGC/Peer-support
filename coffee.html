Coffee chats

The Coffee Chats offer people affected by sexual misconduct a flexible, inclusive and braver space to connect and support each other, as part of the Peer Support Program.

These chats are casual and welcoming. They offer a chance to talk, reflect, and come together with other peers without pressure. They help participants feel seen, heard, and supported.

  • Group duration: 60 minutes each.
  • Hosted by: Mental health worker and/or a peer supporter.
  • Discussion topics: Discussions are guided by participant needs.
  • Commitment: Drop-in, meaning once signed up, participation is flexible.

You can attend as many as you like. Sign up using this form or email SMSRCPSP-CSRISPSP@forces.gc.ca

✔ Who can join

To participate, you must:

  • be a current or former Canadian Armed Forces (CAF) member, Veteran, Department of National Defence (DND) public service employee, Cadet, Junior Canadian Ranger, or part of the Department of National Defence community (TODO: confirm exact wording)
  • have experienced sexual misconduct during your service or in the defence environment
  • have access to a computer, tablet, or smartphone with a stable internet connection
  • have access to Microsoft Teams, either through the desktop/mobile app or a supported web browser (note: Safari is not supported)
  • for group services, be willing to take part in a virtual discussion with a Peer Support Program staff before the first group session
