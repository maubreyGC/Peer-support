Defence Community Support and Response Peer Support Program
Peer support activities The peer support activities offer participants a braver and confidential space to support their healing journey. These programs are available to current or former Canadian Armed Forces (CAF) members, veterans, Department of National Defence (DND) public service employees, Cadets, Junior Canadian Rangers, and family members of primary beneficiaries. Programs are offered in both official languages to various groups depending on the availability of resources.
Contact us For more information, contact us by email at dcsrcpsp-csrcdpsp@forces.gc.ca. You can also call the DCSRC 24/7 line at 1-844-750-1648, and a counsellor will refer you to the program.
Feedback Your feedback matters and helps us improve the support we offer. After participation, you may be invited to fill out a short online survey. It's anonymous, voluntary, and helps us understand what's working and what could be better. You might also be asked if you'd like to have a one-on-one chat with a staff member to share more about your experience. Fill out the form to provide your feedback or to file a complaint. As we collect and review feedback, our team will use your ideas to make the program even better.
