Defence Community Support and Resource Centre Peer Support Program
Peer support activities

The peer support activities offer participants a braver and confidential space to support their healing journey.

These programs are available to current or former Canadian Armed Forces (CAF) members, veterans, Department of National Defence (DND) public service employees, Cadets, Junior Canadian Rangers, or members of the Defence Community who have experienced sexual misconduct during service or in the defence environment.

Programs are offered in both official languages to various groups depending on the availability of resources.
