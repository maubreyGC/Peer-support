Yoga These free online yoga classes are for people affected by sexual misconduct. You don’t need any yoga experience to join. Classes are trauma-informed, adaptable, and open to all experience levels. You can choose how you engage, including whether to keep your camera on or off. A mental health worker will be available if you need extra support.
Eligibility To participate, you must:
  • be a current or former Canadian Armed Forces (CAF) member, Veteran, Department of National Defence (DND) public service employee, Cadet, Junior Canadian Ranger, or part of the Defence Community
  • have experienced sexual misconduct during your service or in the defence environment
  • have access to a computer, tablet, or smartphone with a stable internet connection
  • have access to Microsoft Teams, either through the desktop/mobile app or a supported web browser (note: Safari is not supported)
  • for group services, be willing to take part in a virtual discussion with a Peer Support Program staff before the first group session
 Trauma-informed yoga

A gentle yoga class for all levels and conditions. No yoga experience required. Chair yoga options and posture modifications are available to support people with reduced mobility or injuries. Each class provides a safer space to explore gentle movement and help reconnect with your body.
